UTTARAKHAND TOURIST PLACES

GUN HILL

Gun Hill is one of the highest peaks in Mussoorie and can be easily accessed by a cable car which can be taken from Mall Road. Situated at a height of over 2024 metres, Gun Hill provides a perfect opportunity to spend some quiet time gazing at Doon Valley and the mesmerizing snow-covered peaks of Himalayas
